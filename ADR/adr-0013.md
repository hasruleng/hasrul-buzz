# ADR-0013: Entry flow, revisited — the game world is the default landing; the readable site is the escape

← [ADR index](../ADR.md)

**Status:** Accepted (2026-06-21)
**Supersedes:** [ADR-0006](adr-0006.md) (the readable persona-chooser as the front door)
**Amends:** [ADR-0001](adr-0001.md) (its *front-door arrangement* only; every content-first *guarantee* is kept)
**Extends:** [ADR-0005](adr-0005.md) (the game is the memorability differentiator — now the default surface) ·
[ADR-0008](adr-0008.md) (the world you land in) · [ADR-0012](adr-0012.md) (DOM/React runtime)
**Drivers:** memorability (lead with the differentiator) · product judgment · content-first **guarantees**
preserved (deep-link bypass + skip-to-text always one action away) · movement is load-bearing · honesty (§A)
**Feeds:** [ROADMAP.md](../ROADMAP.md) Phase 2–3

## Context

[ADR-0006](adr-0006.md) made the readable persona-chooser the bare-root front door — content-first by
*presentation*: a visitor sees a calm chooser, and the game is one branch in (welcome → role → branch →
avatar → play). In practice this **buries the differentiator**: the game ([ADR-0005](adr-0005.md) calls
it the memorability differentiator) sits four steps deep, and the bare-root experience reads as a *form*,
not as the game that is supposed to make the site memorable.

Crucially, [ADR-0006](adr-0006.md) already established the fact that flips its own conclusion:

> *"bare-root arrivals are mostly founders/casual/curious, not the time-pressed recruiter (who arrives
> via deep link)."*

If the bare-root visitor is **not** the hurried recruiter — because the recruiter who matters arrives via
a `?persona=…` deep link from a CV/LinkedIn, which bypasses everything — then the bare root can afford to
**be the game**, provided the content-first *guarantees* still hold. That is the move this ADR makes.

This also answers the paired critique that the game "feels like site navigation": part of the cause is
that the game is reached *through* a chooser and presented as a mode you opt into. Making the world the
thing you land in — and **entered by walking, not by clicking a menu** — is half the fix (the rest is
implementation under [ADR-0008](adr-0008.md)).

## Prior art

An entry/landing decision; references are game-first sites and immediate-play patterns.

**Bruno Simon — bruno-simon.com.** Opens *directly* into an interactive (3D driving) experience, with a
fallback/escape for those who don't want it. *Borrowed:* the nerve to make the interactive experience the
**front door**, plus an always-available escape. *Differs / not borrowed:* his is a 3D graphics flex
([ADR-0001](adr-0001.md) rejected that axis); ours is 2D DOM ([ADR-0012](adr-0012.md)) and **content-first
by guarantee** — deep links bypass to text and a skip is always one action away.

**hajj.buzz — dual-mode, the game is the memorable half** (the model in [ADR-0005](adr-0005.md)).
*Borrowed:* leading with the memorable mode. *Differs:* we keep a hard text escape and deep-link bypass so
no one is forced through the game.

**Immediate-play web games / "click to play."** *Borrowed:* drop the visitor into the experience fast,
rather than behind a menu. *Differs:* persona/avatar are picked *in-world*, not on a pre-game form.

**"Skip intro" convention** (cited already in [ADR-0006](adr-0006.md)). *Borrowed and promoted:* the skip
is no longer a corner link behind a chooser — it becomes the **primary, persistent escape to the readable
site**, present from the first frame.

**What we did *not* borrow — and won't.** A forced experience with **no** escape (some game-first sites
trap you); withholding content behind play (breaks [ADR-0001](adr-0001.md)); and 3D/graphics-wow
(reaffirmed rejected).

## Decision

**The bare root (no `?persona=`) opens directly into the game world** ([ADR-0008](adr-0008.md)'s hub),
rendered in DOM/React ([ADR-0012](adr-0012.md)). Persona and avatar are chosen **in-world**, through play,
not on a pre-game form. The readable site stays canonical and is reachable at every moment.

This **supersedes [ADR-0006](adr-0006.md)** (chooser-as-front-door) and **amends
[ADR-0001](adr-0001.md)**'s front-door arrangement — but it preserves all of their content-first
*guarantees*, restated here as the acceptance tests:

1. **Deep links bypass the game entirely.** Any URL with `?persona=` (and `?lens=`) lands **straight on
   the readable view** — the CV/LinkedIn superpower is untouched. The game appears **only** on a bare
   root. *(Unchanged from [ADR-0006](adr-0006.md).)*
2. **Skip-to-text is always ≤1 action and prominent from the first frame** — now the *primary* escape (the
   "magic door" / "read the site"), not a buried corner link. Content-first becomes a **guarantee**
   (always one action away) rather than a statement about what you see first.
3. **The game never withholds content.** The readable site holds every fact unconditionally
   ([ADR-0001](adr-0001.md)).
4. **The avatar stays cosmetic** (§A) — no visitor attribute is asked for or recorded. *(Carried from
   [ADR-0006](adr-0006.md).)*
5. **Movement is load-bearing, not decorative.** You enter and traverse the world by *walking*;
   encounters are approached, persona emerges through play. This is the direct answer to the
   "movement feels pointless / it's just navigation" critique — movement is the entry, not a garnish.
6. **An accessible non-movement path always exists** — the skip-to-text escape plus keyboard/click
   affordances, and `prefers-reduced-motion` honored, so a game-default never traps anyone who can't or
   won't play.

**Why this is safe** (the [ADR-0006](adr-0006.md) logic, completed): the visitor who must reach facts in
seconds is the deep-linked recruiter — and they never see the game. The bare-root visitor is the
founder/casual/curious, for whom the game *is* the right first impression — and even they can skip to text
in one action. So leading with the game raises memorability without breaking the content-first promise to
the people who need speed.

## Alternatives considered

- **Keep [ADR-0006](adr-0006.md)'s chooser as the front door** — content-first by presentation, lowest
  risk, already built. *Rejected by Hasrul:* it buries the differentiator and reads as a form, not a game.
- **In-world *opening* but still a gate before the game** (a cinematic intro you click through) — warmer
  than a form, but still defers the game and adds a step. *Rejected — half-measure.*
- **Game default with NO text escape** (force the world) — maximal commitment to the game, but breaks
  content-first and abandons the hurried bare-root visitor. *Rejected — violates [ADR-0001](adr-0001.md).*
- **Game default, persona chosen on a form first, then walk** — keeps a form. *Rejected — the point is to
  choose in-world; the form is what felt un-game-like.*

## Consequences

- **`App.tsx` routing flips:** a bare root (no persona) mounts the **game world**, not the chooser. The
  deep-link branches (`?persona=`/`?lens=` → readable views) are **unchanged**.
- **[ADR-0006](adr-0006.md)'s chooser steps move in-world or are replaced** — persona and avatar are
  chosen during play (e.g. Hasrul as a host NPC asks "what's your quest?", or the visitor walks toward one
  of a few gates). Exact staging is implementation under [ADR-0008](adr-0008.md) scene ①–②.
- **A prominent, persistent "read the site / skip to text" control is owed** — visible from the first
  frame, the primary escape. This is the magic door promoted to top-level.
- **Movement becomes load-bearing** ([ADR-0008](adr-0008.md)): the world is entered by walking; content is
  encountered in-world rather than opened from a menu (the modal-panel hub was the navigation smell). A
  camera/exploration feel and approach-triggered encounters are owed — the concrete #2 fix.
- **[ADR-0006](adr-0006.md) is marked Superseded; [ADR-0001](adr-0001.md)'s front-door arrangement is
  amended** (guarantees intact, not its substance).
- **Risk:** a bare-root recruiter *without* a deep link now lands in the game — mitigated by (1) the
  deep-link bypass for the recruiters who matter and (2) the always-present one-action skip.
- **Supersede trigger:** if analytics show bare-root bounce rises or reach-the-content rate drops versus
  the chooser, revert the front door — recorded as a new ADR, never an in-place edit here.
